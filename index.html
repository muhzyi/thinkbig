Filmography

Director and writer of incredibly profound films for over 25 years, James Cameron has a way of connecting with audiences whilst simultaneously making his own dreams come to fruition. Oftentimes referenced as a visionary, and highly creative, he is known for creating such classic, blockbuster movies as Titanic, Terminator, Avatar, and Aliens. As we all know, these are the kinds of movies that stick around for generations to come with people quoting and referencing the many fabulous elements each has to offer. Besides being what we know him to be, he is also a movie industry innovator who started his own companies that strive to create new ways to capture great stories on film.
